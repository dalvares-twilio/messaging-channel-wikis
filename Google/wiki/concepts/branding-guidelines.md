## Overview

RBM agents display branding elements (logo, hero image, colors) in messaging apps. Proper formatting ensures professional appearance and accessibility.

## Details

### Logo Requirements

- **Size**: 224x224 px, ≤50 kB
- **Display**: cropped to 224px diameter circle
- Leave space for circular cropping — don't fill entire canvas
- Space logo equidistant from all edges
- Avoid transparency (poor dark mode visibility) — use white background if needed

### Hero/Banner Image

- **Size**: 1440x448 px (45:14 aspect ratio), ≤200 kB
- Logo partially overlaps hero image — design accordingly
- Incorrect aspect ratio causes cropping issues

### Color

- Must have minimum **4.5:1 contrast ratio to white** (WCAG 2.0 accessibility)
- Use tools like WebAIM Contrast Checker to verify
- Example passing color: #E91B0C
