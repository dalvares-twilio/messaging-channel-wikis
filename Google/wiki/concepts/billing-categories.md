## Overview

RBM agents are assigned a billing category that reflects their interaction pattern with users. This affects how messaging costs are calculated.

## Details

### Two Categories

1. **Conversational** — For agents engaging in multi-turn conversations with users
2. **Non-conversational** — For agents sending messages without expecting frequent replies (notifications, alerts)

### Mutability

- Can only change billing category **before creating** the agent
- Post-launch changes require contacting RCS for Business support team

### Selection Criteria

- Choose based on expected interaction pattern
- Conversational: customer support, interactive services
- Non-conversational: notifications, OTPs, one-way alerts
