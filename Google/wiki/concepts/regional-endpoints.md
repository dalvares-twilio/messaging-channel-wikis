## Overview

RBM agents operate from one of three regional endpoints. The region determines where the agent operates and stores data, but does not restrict carrier access.

## Details

### Three Regions

1. **North America**
2. **Europe**
3. **Asia Pacific**

### Selection Criteria

- Choose based on applicable regulations and requirements
- Consider proximity to end users for latency
- **All regions have equal access to carriers worldwide**

### Proximity Guidance

| Your Location | Recommended Region |
|---------------|-------------------|
| Latin America | North America |
| Africa | Europe |
| Australia | Asia Pacific |

### Immutability

Region **cannot be changed** after agent creation — choose carefully.
