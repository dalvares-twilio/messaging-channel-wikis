## Summary

When an agent is ready for users, it must go through a two-step launch process: brand verification and launch approval. Launches are either Google-managed or carrier-managed, with different requirements for each.

## Key Points

- Launch process: (1) Brand verification → (2) Launch approval
- After approval, agent can reach RBM-capable users on approved carrier networks
- **Google-managed**: Google handles brand verification and launch approval
- **Carrier-managed**: Carrier directly manages verification and approval; requires direct agreement with carrier
- Carriers marked with info icon in Developer Console = carrier-managed
- Carrier-managed launches are rejected if no contract in place
