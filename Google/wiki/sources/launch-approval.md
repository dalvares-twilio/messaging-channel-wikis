## Summary

Launch approval involves review of brand verification and agent assets by Google, carriers, or both. Google-managed launches typically take 1-3 business days if requirements are met.

## Key Points

### Prerequisites
- Fill out all agent information
- Test agent functionality
- Implement STOP (opt-out) flow
- Create agent preview video (publicly accessible)

### Launch Request Info Needed
- Countries and carriers list
- Points of contact (name, email, phone)
- How opt-in is obtained (optional)
- Message triggers and interaction types
- Opt-out response message
- Review instructions or videos

### Timeline
- Google-managed: **1-3 business days** if:
  - Brand approval within 24 hours
  - Working website, Privacy Policy, ToS links
  - Agent preview video
- After approval: agent reachable in ~30 minutes

### Brand Approval Email
- For Google-managed launches: brand POC receives email from `rbm-support@google.com`
- Subject: "Please approve brand use for RCS Business Messaging agent: AGENT_ID"
- Response is **required** — faster response = faster approval
