## Summary

Brand verification confirms that an authorized brand representative verifies agent information and the partner's right to manage the agent. This is a prerequisite for launch and happens after submitting a launch request.

## Key Points

- Required before first launch submission
- Submit once per agent (regardless of how many carriers)
- **Brand contact**: employee with authority to verify agent info
- **Supporting documents**: PDF files (up to 5, max 50MB each) like authorization letters
- Verification happens **after** launch request submission
- **Google-managed launches**: brand contacted once, applies to all future Google-managed launches
- **Carrier-managed launches**: brand may be contacted by each carrier separately
- Cannot edit verification info after submitting launch request
