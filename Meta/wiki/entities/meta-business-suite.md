## Description

Meta Business Suite (formerly Facebook Business Manager) is Meta's web-based platform for managing business assets including WhatsApp Business Accounts, phone numbers, and partner relationships.

## URL

https://business.facebook.com/

## Key Functions

| Function | Path |
|----------|------|
| WhatsApp accounts | Settings > Accounts > WhatsApp accounts |
| Partners | Settings > Users > Partners |
| Business info | Settings > Business info |

## WABA Operations

- Create new WABA
- View WABA details
- Assign partners (up to 2)
- Access WhatsApp Manager

## Partner Management

- Share WABAs with solution providers
- Define partner permissions
- Partner must share credit line for messaging

## Contact Book Settings

- Enable/disable contact book
- Path: Business settings > Business info
