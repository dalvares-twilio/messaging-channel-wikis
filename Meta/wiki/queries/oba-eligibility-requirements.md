## Question

What are the eligibility requirements to obtain Official Business Account (OBA) status and the blue checkmark on WhatsApp?

## Answer

To be eligible for OBA status (blue checkmark), a business must meet all of these criteria:

1. **Policy Compliance** — Comply with WhatsApp Business Messaging Policy
2. **Platform Tenure** — Registered on the platform for at least 30 days
3. **Notability** — Represent a notable, well-known brand (based on presence in news articles from major publications)
4. **Business Verification** — Business portfolio verified through Meta's Business Verification process
5. **Two-Step Verification** — Enabled on the phone number
6. **Display Name Approved** — Display name must be verified

**Important Notes:**
- Paid or promotional content is NOT considered for notability assessment
- Previous OBA approvals do NOT guarantee approval for other phone numbers
- If denied, must wait **30 days** before reapplying
- Not granted to: employees, test accounts, or WhatsApp Business app numbers
- Non-OBA numbers do not appear in WhatsApp search results

**For sub-brands:** Use format `{{sub-brand}} by {{notable parent}}` to leverage parent brand notability.
