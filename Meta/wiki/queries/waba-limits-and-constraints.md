## Question

What are the limits and constraints when creating and managing WhatsApp Business Accounts (WABAs)?

## Answer

WhatsApp Business Accounts have several key limitations:

**Account Limits:**
- **Max 20 WABAs** per Meta Business Account
- **Max 250 templates** per WABA
- **Max 2 phone numbers** initially per WABA (can increase to 20)

**Ownership Constraints:**
- A WABA belongs to only **one Business Manager** (single owner)
- **Cannot migrate** a WABA between businesses once a credit line is attached
- **Time zone and currency** are locked after credit line attachment

**Partner Sharing:**
- Can share a WABA with up to **2 partners**
- Partner must share credit line before you can send messages
