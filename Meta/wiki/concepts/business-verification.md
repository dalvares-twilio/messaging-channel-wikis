## Overview

Business verification is the process of verifying a business portfolio (formerly Business Manager) with Meta. Verification is required for certain platform features, including Official Business Account (OBA) eligibility.

## Requirements for OBA

To be eligible for OBA status, your business portfolio must be verified through Meta's Business Verification process.

## Verification Status

Returned by WABA API as `business_verification_status`:
- `verified` — verification complete
- `pending` — verification in progress
- Other statuses indicate issues
