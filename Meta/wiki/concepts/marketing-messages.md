## Definition

Marketing messages are promotional template messages sent to WhatsApp users. Users can opt out of receiving marketing messages from specific businesses.

## User Preferences

WhatsApp users can control their marketing message preferences:
- **Stop** — Opt out of marketing messages from a business
- **Resume** — Opt back in to receive marketing messages

## Template Categories

Marketing is one of three template categories:
- `MARKETING` — Promotional content
- `UTILITY` — Transactional/service updates
- `AUTHENTICATION` — Verification codes

## Related Webhooks

- `user_preferences` — Notifies when users change marketing preferences
